Where's My Doctor?

A multi-platform application that empowers the people in the Philippines to find a doctor who can help them based on their symptoms and preferences (location, HMO, budget, etc.) at the tip of their fingers /at their own convenience.

The application allows them to set an appointment using their phone or through the web app. Our ultimate goal is to provide proper and reliable healthcare information right at their fingertips.
